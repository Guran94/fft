11/26/1979

Yesterday they ordered us to a small intersection names Provins and built a camp there. Fortunately I have to not serve like guard by the Marshal law anymore and now we have got new orderes. I drew on a map the guerilla territory and reflected in my mind under that circumstances it was acquired.

The war has not ended already, although we have new task. Our HQ thinks, that the rebels want to strike on the central Everon because of their hidding camps, but not so hidding, we know it already. They've got some armored vehicles there so we will take care of it although still we must to avoid the forests, even through sergeant Muday offered to HQ, that we would risk it and go through out the forest south west from the first hostile camp to not go round it and to have better cover behind the trees. He is so stupid idiot! Doesn't he? But HQ has accepted. What should I say?
Perhaps the command is right, and rebels troops are holding their leaders nicely in the three towns and pissing on the forests, but we'd better be careful not to hit any of the forest patrols.


We only endure defeats on Everon, today we will try to turn this series around.
The reacon company has discovered two hidden enemy camps and by its reports the rebels have got a several Russian tanks. The task is simple - destroy them earlier then they attack the central Everon.

You start in Provins. Due to the lack of APCs a truck will take your squad on the mission and unload you by the near forest.



Tasks

1) First eliminate the enemy resist in camp number one and destroy one T55.

2) Then move to camp number two and take care of two BMPs there.


Tanks destroyed


Nice work! Since the outbreak of the war, we have celebrated the first win here!
